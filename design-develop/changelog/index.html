Changelog for Design and Develop Overview

About

This page is designed so translators can copy and paste from this rendered changelog page. Or, translators can use the Design and Develop Overview page source.

For others, particularly significant or substantive changes are summarized after “Significant:”.

Page Contents
  • 19 May 2022
  • 1 April 2022 Significant

19 May 2022

  • Under “Other Design and Development Resources” heading, second item, changed link from:
    [WAI-ARIA Authoring Practices](https://www.w3.org/TR/wai-aria-practices/)
    to:
    [WAI-ARIA Authoring Practices Guide (APG)](/WAI/ARIA/apg/)

1 April 2022 Significant

Significant: Added link to Cognitive Accessibility Guidance.

  • Under “Other Design and Development Resources” heading, added new third bullet:

[Cognitive Accessibility Guidance](/WAI/WCAG2/supplemental/#cognitiveaccessibilityguidance)
: Provides objecties and design patterns to improve accessibility for people with cognitive and learning disabilitie. Introduced in [About Supplemental Guidance](/WAI/WCAG2/supplemental/about/).
